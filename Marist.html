Welcome,Tēnā Koutou Katoa to Marist College

Marist College is a vibrant and diverse yet firmly grounded in its ethos of Catholicism and family.

Our school offers a broad and balanced year 7 - 13 curiculm, and we are very proud of student's outstanding achievement rates in National Qualifications.

Situated in Mt Albert, Auckland, Marist College has been at its maximum roll of 750 students for the past ten years.

History Of Marist College

Marist College was founded in 1928 by the Marist Sisters, a congregation of Roman Catholic Women.

Started in France in 1817 by Jeanne Marie Chavoin and Jean Claude Colin.

The College had 3 names: Marist Convent High School, Marist Sisters College then finally Marist College(1996)
